Scene 5 • Ingenuity

How AI Scrubber Reimagines the Workflow

Highlight the clever AI moments that go beyond “just automation”.

“AI Scrubber starts with autonomous file intake: it detects new files from Jarvis and scrubbing automatically—no manual downloading or uploading.”

“It performs smart data validation, fixing ZIP codes and dates, and only flags truly ambiguous rows.”

“For invalid records, it looks up the mover in Jarvis, compares fields, and suggests the correct rejection reason.”

“It syncs mover changes in real time, reads Zendesk tickets for last-minute updates, and finally exports and delivers the CSV to FTP and prepares the IMS email.”

AI Capabilities
  • Monitoring & ingestion of files.
  • Structured data repair (ZIPs, dates, formats).
  • Record lookup + mismatch detection.
  • Classification of invalid reasons.
  • Cross-system reasoning (Jarvis + Zendesk).
  • Workflow orchestration to FTP & IMS.
